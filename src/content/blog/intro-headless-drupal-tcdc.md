Four Kitchens offered its popular Introduction to Headless Drupal class free of charge at Twin Cities Drupal Camp 2015. The hands-on, code-all-day training taught attendees how to use Drupal as a world-class content API and integrate it with Node.js to build rich, interactive digital experiences. The curriculum covered reading and writing data to Drupal, creating custom API endpoints, working with the Express framework, understanding NPM, and writing non-blocking asynchronous code. If you're interested in the foundations of decoupled Drupal development, [read the full article](https://www.fourkitchens.com/blog/article/intro-headless-drupal-tcdc/).
