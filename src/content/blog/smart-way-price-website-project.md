Website projects, much like home renovations, rarely go exactly as planned — and how you price them makes all the difference. This article breaks down three common pricing models: Time & Materials, Fixed Bid, and Fixed Price with Variable Scope, explaining why the third option offers the best balance of predictability and flexibility. The "fixed price with variable scope" model protects launch dates while allowing teams to incorporate new learnings at every project phase — from discovery through post-launch. The article walks through how understanding deepens at each stage (discovery, design, development, and launch), and why that's a feature, not a bug. If you're planning a website redesign and want to avoid budget overruns and missed deadlines, [read the full article](https://www.fourkitchens.com/blog/digital-strategy/smart-way-price-website-project/).
